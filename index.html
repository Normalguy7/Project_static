Private Health Insurance: Protect Yourself from Waiting Lists.

The NZ Healthcare Trade-Off

In emergency situations, New Zealand health care is great, but when it comes to the non-urgent issues, you either go on a wait list or pay privately. When you have an issue that needs attention, neither of these options are appealing.

Many providers let you build your own cover to ensure you only pay for what you truly need.

Start My Free Cover Assessment

Tailor-Made Coverage Options

Your policy can include specialized options such as:

  • Cover 100% of the costs
  • Non-Pharmac drug cover (drugs not funded by the public system)
  • Specialist and tests (faster diagnosis and treatment planning)
  • Surgical treatment (bypassing public waiting lists)
  • Non-surgical treatment
  • Before and after hospital costs (consultations, rehabilitation)
  • Medical tourism option (fly to another country for specialist treatment)
